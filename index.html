Who We Are

A Rookie Team. A Big Vision.

Mage Robotics is a new FTC team from Kanata, Ontario. We're a group of passionate students committed to learning, building, and competing, with ambitions that go far beyond our first season.

Our Story

From Kanata, For the Future

We're a first-year FTC team, f by students who believe robotics is more than a competition: it's a launchpad for engineers, designers, strategists, and leaders of tomorrow.

Every bolt tightened and line of code written is a step toward something larger. We're here to build, to learn, and to leave our mark.

Our Values in Action

Why It Matters

We believe robotics is one of the most powerful ways to unlock potential in young people. Our team is committed to making this space inclusive, rigorous, and inspiring. Not just for ourselves, but for the community around us.

From soft skills developed in the build room to the technical fluency gained through code and CAD, every student on Mage Robotics leaves with more than they came in with.
